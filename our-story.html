Our Story

"WHERE DREAMS COME TO LIFE"

Hanzo Films is an innovative production powerhouse and animation studio ,spanning three dynamic locations, the UAE, Singapore, and India. Since our inception in 2012, we’ve cultivated a reputation for crafting captivating narratives that defy convention. Noteworthy among our achievements is the ‘Give in to Giving’ commercial for Emirates NBD, recognized globally for its distinctive storytelling. Our diverse portfolio features collaborations with esteemed clients such as Emirates Group, Etihad Airways, IKEA, Cadbury International, Bridgestone , HSBC, Nissan, Kellogg’s, Careem, GMC, McDonald’s, Evian, and many others. Hanzo Films prides itself on pushing the envelope, imbuing every project with creativity, passion and excellence, ensuring our work resonates authentically with audiences.
